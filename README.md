Project Description: Problem: Small and emerging brands often struggle to find affordable and relevant influencers for marketing, while micro-influencers face difficulty in getting consistent collaboration opportunities and visibility. Existing platforms are either expensive or focus mainly on large influencers, leaving a gap in the market.

Solution: Our platform, Collabify, bridges the gap between brands and micro-influencers by providing a centralized hub where both can connect, collaborate, and grow. Brands can easily discover influencers based on niche and engagement, while influencers can showcase their profiles and apply for campaigns seamlessly

Key Features: 🔍 Smart search and filtering to find influencers by niche, followers, and engagement 📢 Campaign posting system for brands to create and manage collaborations 🤝 Collaboration management with application tracking and approval system 💬 Real-time chat for direct communication and negotiation ⭐ Ratings and reviews for transparency and trust

Tech Stack: React.js, Node.js / FastAPI, MongoDB / PostgreSQL, JWT Authentication, Socket.IO

Impact: This platform empowers micro-influencers by giving them equal opportunities to grow and earn, while helping small businesses run cost-effective marketing campaigns. It promotes a more inclusive and efficient digital marketing ecosystem
